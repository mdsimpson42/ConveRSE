There are many resources available to provide information and support for mental health issues.

Emergency Support

If you are in immediate danger, call 999 or go to your nearest Accident and Emergency department.

You can also:

  • Call Samaritans on 116 123 (UK-wide)
  • Text SHOUT to 85258 (UK-wide)
  • Call C.A.L.L. on 0800 132 737 (Wales)

You can also call 111 or speak to your GP.

More Information and Support

There are many organisations that can provide information and support for mental health issues.

Here are a few:

Organisation Services
Mind Mind have a range of information, including an A-Z of Mental Health, as well as access to tools and services.
NHS The National Health Service (NHS) provides a range of mental health services in the UK.
CALM The Campaign Against Living Miserably (CALM) provides information and hosts a webchat service and a private online forum.
Hub of Hope Hub of Hope allows you to search for local, national, peer, community, charity, private and NHS mental health support/
MindOut MindOut is an organisation that provides specialised services run by and for LGBTQ+ people
