You can contribute to this site in a number of ways, which are detailed below.

Blog Submissions

We would like to encourage as many people, from a wide range of backgrounds, to contribute to the blog. You could share your mental health story, talk about your experiences with mental health in academia, or discuss how you have supported others. You could share tips and advice, or links to resources that you have found helpful.

There are two ways to submit a blog post.

Pull Request

You can add a blog post to the site via a pull reuqest on GitHub.

  • Step 1 - create a branch/fork of the repository.
  • Step 2 - add your blog post (plus author profile, tags and images) to the code.
  • Step 3 - commit your changes and create a pull request to merge them onto the main branch.
  • Step 4 - your changes will be deployed automatically once the pull request has been approved.
